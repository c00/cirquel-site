Cirquel User Agreement
======================

The purpose of Cirquel

Cirquel is meant to be a crowdsourced library of Circus tricks and poses. It's a resource 
for everyone in the community to learn. It should be an open, motivating and friendly 
platform. Moderators will actively work on keeping the platform that way.

Community and posting guidelines

Any form of bad behavior such as bullying, bad language, inappropriate content, sexually 
explicit content, etc. is prohibited. We will remove such content. Repeat offenders will 
have their accounts disabled.

If you find any inappropriate content, use the "report" button, or send an email to 
abuse@cirquelapp.com.
